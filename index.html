    My name is Joseph Robinette Biden Junior but you probably know me as Joe Biden.
    I am the 46th and current president of the United States (since 20/01/2021), and a member of the Democratic Party.
    I am 79 years old,I was born on November 20, 1942 and raised in Scranton, Pennsylvania.
    My wife's name is Jill. We got married in 1997.
    I have 4 children: Beau, Hunter, Naomi and Ashley. I also have 2 dogs.
    I love baseball and football, and was one of the leading scorers on our undefeated football team during my senior year.

    Question Number 1

    Web 1.0 Web 2.0 Web 3.0
    Reading only Reading and writing Reading, writing and executing
    Companies publish content that people consume People publish content that other people can consume, companies build platforms that let people publish content for other people People build applications that people can interact with, companies build platforms that let people publish services by leveraging the associations between people or special content
    Web 1.0 was all about static content, one way publishing of content without any real interaction between readers or publishers or each other. Web 2.0 is more about 2 way communication through social networking, blogging, wikis, tagging, user generated content and video. Web 3.0 is curiusly undefined. AI and the web learning what you want and delivering you a Personalized web experience
    In Web 1.0 search engines retrieve macro contents. Search is very fast but many times results are inaccurate In Web 2.0 search engines retrieve tags with micro contents. The process of tagging is manual, tedious and covers negligible precents of the web In Web 3.0 search engines will hopefully retrieve micro content texts which were tagged automatically. This implies translating billions of Web 1.0 macro contents into micro contents. The result could be more precise search because tagging can solve part of the ambiguity that homonyms and synonyms introduce into the process of Search.
